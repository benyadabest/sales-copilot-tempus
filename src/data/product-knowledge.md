# Tempus Oncology Test Portfolio

## Somatic / Treatment Selection

### xT CDx
- Type: FDA-approved 648-gene solid tumor NGS panel
- Specimen: FFPE tissue + matched normal (blood/saliva)
- Turnaround: 10-14 calendar days from specimen receipt
- Detects: SNVs, MNVs, INDELs, MSI status, TMB, HLA Class I, CNVs, rearrangements (22 genes)
- CDx claims: Colorectal cancer (CRC)
- Key stat: 88.6% of patients had ≥1 biologically relevant alteration
- Key stat: 96% of patients matched to a clinical trial when clinical data combined with NGS
- Differentiator: Tumor + normal matched approach reduces somatic false-positives vs tumor-only
- Report includes: Therapy matching (OncoKB + NCCN), clinical trial matching, Tempus One AI assistant

### xR
- Type: Whole-transcriptome RNA sequencing
- Specimen: FFPE tissue
- Covers: 100+ targeted fusion genes, splice variants (MET exon 14, EGFRvIII)
- Key stat: 29% more patients identified with unique clinically actionable fusions vs DNA alone
- Differentiator: Unbiased detection — not limited to predefined fusion partners

### xF (Liquid Biopsy)
- Type: 105-gene ctDNA liquid biopsy panel
- Specimen: Blood draw
- Detects: SNVs, INDELs, CNVs, MSI
- Use case: When tissue unavailable, monitoring resistance mutations
- Key stat: 9% of patients had unique actionable alterations in liquid biopsy not found in tissue

### xF+
- Type: 523-gene expanded liquid biopsy panel
- Additional: Clonal hematopoiesis (CH) variant detection
- Use case: Comprehensive liquid profiling, resistance monitoring (lung, breast, ovarian, CRC)

### CancerNext-Expanded® (xG+)
- Type: 77-gene expanded germline panel

### BRCAplus®

### +RNAinsight®
